Chipper Cash

2022 - Current

Chipper Cash is a financial technology company that provides cross-border remittance payment solutions across Africa.

At Chipper Cash, I worked as a senior product designer and lead a ton of high impact initiatives like the design of the API merchant dashboard, redesign of the Onboarding and verification flows, Zambian Launch in partnership with Western Union, amongst others.

Merchant API Dashboard – unlocked $6B+ in transactions

When merchants are integrating with our API, they relied solely on the sales team to onboard, generate API keys, and view transactions. This not only slowed down growth and overwhelmed the sales teams, it also made it difficult for us to onboard high-value merchants faster.

I designed and launched a self-serve dashboard that gave merchants full control to generate keys, monitor transactions, and manage accounts with little or no human intervention.

This reduced the sales team's workload, reduced onboarding time, and helped power over $6B in API-driven transactions to date.

Redesigned KYC flow – saved $800K in vendor fees

KYC Flow Image

When we needed to streamline our vendor costs, I led two critical redesigns of ChipperCash's KYC flow. First, replacing our third-party Veriface vendor with an in-house facial recognition model, saving the company over $800K annually. And much later, I optimized the entire flow for speed and clarity across markets, resulting in a 2% uplift in verification completion. Together, these efforts streamlined onboarding, saved costs and reduced drop-off rates.

USD virtual accounts & payment links – powered $5M in global payments

USD Accounts Image

Today, millions of Nigerians lack easy access to receive international payments. I saw the opportunity and pitched an idea: launch USD virtual accounts bundled with USD payment links. This will give users U.S. bank details and an ability to simply request and receive money globally (in USD and stable coins).

The proposal was approved, and I led the rollout of this cross-border payments feature. It helped Chipper reposition as a platform for freelancers and creators not just peer-to-peer transfers.

Since launch, we've issued over 15,000 USD accounts, processed 20,000 payment links and $5M+ in transactions.

Zambian launch with Western Union

Zambian Launch Image

Chipper has long wanted to expand into Zambia, this led to their acquisition of Zoona in 2023. Now this was finally happening, and we secured a partnership with Western Union to power our cross-border payments.

The challenge was designing a locally intuitive flow for a market I'd never set foot in, while meeting both partner requirements and user expectations.

I worked closely with Zambian colleagues to uncover on-the-ground user behaviors and needs. I reviewed and mirrored existing competitors in the market and translated all of those into a seamless experience that resonated.

The launch marked our successful entry into Zambia and laid the foundation for regional expansion in partnership with a global brand.
