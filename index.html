Hi, I'm Tee

Senior Product Designer with 7 years of experience. Using a systems-thinking approach, I translate complex challenges into intuitive, high-performing solutions that drive significant business growth.

My work has delivered multi-million dollar impacts, including a FinTech API processing over $6B and a verification feature that saved $800K+ in vendor costs. I specialize in leading projects from concept to launch, collaborating with product and engineering to build great solutions.

Personal Values

  • - Ship valuable work over perfect designs.
  • - The design process is never linear.
  • - Always Iterate; use curiosity to improve yourself.
  • - Make deliberate choices. Foster a trusted space for diverse perspectives to hone your thoughts.
  • - Share early and often. Bring people along with you.

Life outside work

When I am not buried in design work, I am probably at the gym chasing my next PR, getting lost in the streets of new cities and countries, or hunting down the coziest cafes I can find.

Lately, my evenings have been spent unraveling the startup chaos and tech drama in Silicon Valley. I also love a good story in the form of books especially when it's about design or finance or from the rich tapestry of African literature.

I'm a firm believer in the power of sharing knowledge. I do this either through talks, long-form thoughts on Substack and Medium, or one-on-one coaching sessions with designers.
