Fortune 500 - Battery intelligence

As the sole designer on a 3-year project for a Fortune 500 client, I led the UX redesign of BDMS, an internal tool for requesting, monitoring and analyzing battery performance tests.

At the beginning of the project, we weren't allowed to speak to users, and early designs were based solely on internal assumptions and client knowledge of the tool.

When we released some key features, I recognized the impact of this and led a full discovery phase; conducting interviews, remote shadowing, knowledge sharing sessions and workshops to map the end-to-end battery testing workflow.

This led to a better understanding of our users and how they used the tool in their work. It also influenced how the client worked internally, other PMs within the product adopted our approach. We created a Battery Process Map, which aligned stakeholders, and uncovered some gaps. The redesigned system helped streamline workflows, simplify battery test setup, and made complex data easier to interpret.

The impact: battery cell and testengineers saved hundreds of hours annually, boosting confidence and productivity. The team also delivered a scalable foundation for future innovation.

One engineer summed it up best:
"The changes to the cloning interface(a step in battery test creation) cut my setup time in half."

Verizon - Resource mgt app for on-prem cluster

As part of a Verizon–NVIDIA–Monks initiative, I joined as the sole designer on a 12-week sprint to shape Ceres, a next-gen platform for deploying AI media apps on private 5G. The infrastructure was built, but my role was to make it usable for creative partners without DevOps backgrounds.

I designed key beta workflows:
→ Multi-tenant partner management.
→ Tools for partner app deployment and lifecycle management
→ Functionality for deploying and controlling sandbox environments
→ UI integration of the NVIDIA NIM library to support AI model access

turning complex infrastructure into accessible tools.

This foundation now powers live AI pipelines and on-site media workflows across Verizon’s private 5G network.
