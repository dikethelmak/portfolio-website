Monks

2022 - 2024

Monks is a global digital-first agency that creates tech and marketing solutions for top clients. Their clients include Google, Meta, Amazon, Walmart, etc

In a hybrid consultant/designer role, I worked directly with clients at Apple, Verizon and Equifax to gather requirements and create designs that redefined their goals.

Apple - Battery intelligence

Apple Battery Intelligence Image

As the sole designer on a 3-year project for a Fortune 500 client, I led the UX redesign of BDMS, an internal tool for monitoring and analyzing battery performance.

At the beginning of the project, we weren't allowed to speak to users, and early designs were based solely on internal assumptions and client knowledge of the tool.

When we released some key features, I recognized the impact of this and pushed for a full discovery phase; conducting interviews, remote shadowing, knowledge sharing sessions and workshops to map the end-to-end battery testing workflow.

This led to a better understanding of our users and how they used the tool in their work. It also influenced how the client worked internally, other PMs within the product adopted our approach, and the client trusted the creation of a Battery Process Map, which aligned stakeholders, uncovered critical gaps, and restored trust in the design process. The redesigned system helped streamline fragmented workflows, simplified test setup, and made complex data easier to interpret.

The impact: engineers saved hundreds of hours annually, confidence and productivity improved, and the team delivered a scalable foundation for future innovation.

One engineer summed it up best:
"The changes to the cloning interface cut my setup time in half."
